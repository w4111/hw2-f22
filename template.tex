All relational algebra symbols:
Projection: $\pi$, Selection: $\sigma$, Renaming: $\rho$, Join: $\bowtie$, Cross Product: $\times$, Union: $\cup$, Difference: $-$, Intersection: $\cap$, and: $\wedge$, or: $\vee$.\\
